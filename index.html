Our Story

One rainy evening in Bangalore, a young tea‑lover named Parmesh discovered the magic of a steaming cup of chai served from a humble stall. The warmth and aroma sparked a simple idea: "What if we could bring that feeling to every corner of the city?"

After years of traveling India's tea‑growing regions, Parmesh perfected a blend that balances spice and sweetness. The result is Parmesh Chai Point – a space where every cup tells a story.

Join us, sip with us, and let's spread the love for chai together.
