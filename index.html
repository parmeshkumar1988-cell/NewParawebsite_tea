Our Story

One rainy evening in Bangalore, a young tea‑lover named Parmesh discovered the magic of a steaming cup of chai served from a humble stall. The warmth, the aroma, and the brief conversation with the stall‑owner sparked a simple idea: “What if we could bring that feeling to every corner of the city?”

After years of traveling India’s tea‑growing regions, learning from farmers, and perfecting the blend, Parmesh gathered a small team to turn the idea into reality. The result is Parmesh Chai Point – a space where every cup tells a story, where people can pause, share, and feel at home.

We need your support. Whether it’s a visit, a word‑of‑mouth, or a simple “thank you”, every bit helps us keep the tradition alive. Join us, sip with us, and let’s spread the love for chai together.
